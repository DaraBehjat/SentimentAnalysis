

LONDON  Nov 30 (Reuters) - One person has been confirmed
dead after a police helicopter crashed into a busy Scottish pub
and the death toll is expected to rise, police said on Saturday. 
The helicopter crashed into the roof of the Clutha pub in
Glasgow at 10:25 on Friday night. "I can also confirm one fatality. We expect that number to
increase over the coming hours," Stephen House, chief constable
of Police Scotland told reporters.
