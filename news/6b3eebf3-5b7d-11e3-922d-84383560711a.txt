

RIO DE JANEIRO  Dec 2 (Reuters) - Brazilian global mining
group Vale SA  expects the global iron ore
supply to rise, starting in 2014, and the international market
will be able to absorb the increase, the company's iron ore
chief, Jose Carlos Martins, told investors in New York on
Monday.
