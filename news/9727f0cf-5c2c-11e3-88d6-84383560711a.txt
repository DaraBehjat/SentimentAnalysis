

Dec 3 (Reuters) - Questerre 
* The company has entered into an agreement with a syndicate
of underwriters to purchase, on a guaranteed agency basis, 5.1
million Common Shares, to be issued on a flow-through basis 
(the "CEE Flow-Through Shares"), at $1.55 per CEE Flow-Through
Share for aggregate gross proceeds of $7.905 million. * The gross proceeds of the CEE Flow-Through Shares will 
be used by the Company to incur eligible Canadian exploration
expenses ("Qualifying Expenditures") prior to December 31, 2014
on Questerre's properties. * The Company will renounce the Qualifying Expenditures to 
subscribers of the CEE Flow-Through Shares for the fiscal year
ended December 31, 2013
