

U.S. agribusiness giant Cargill is building a $100
million cocoa processing facility in Gresik, East Java, with a
capacity of 70,000 tonnes per year, said Job Leuning, the
company's head of Head of Cocoa and Chocolate for Asia Pacific
Region. He added that the plant is expected to start operations
in mid-2014.
