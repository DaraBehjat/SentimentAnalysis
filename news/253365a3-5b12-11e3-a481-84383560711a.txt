

PT Pertamina Irak Eksplorasi Produksi, a unit of state oil and
gas firm PT Pertamina, has finalized a deal to buy 10
percent shares in Exxon Mobil Iraq Limited in West
Qurna-1 block, Iraq, which currently produces 500,000 barrels of
oil per day, Investor Daily reports. The block is estimated to
have oil reserves of about 9 billion barrels. 
* Note: Reuters has not verified this story and does not
vouch for its accuracy.
