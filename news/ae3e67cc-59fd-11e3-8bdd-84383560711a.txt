

RIO DE JANEIRO  Nov 29 (Reuters) - MMX Mineração e Metálicos
SA, the Brazilian iron ore miner controlled by tycoon
Eike Batista, said on Friday that it recorded a third-quarter
net loss of 1.21 bln reais. 
The loss compares with a 100.6 million real loss in the same
period a year earlier.
