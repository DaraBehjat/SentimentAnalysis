

NEW YORK  Dec 12 (Reuters) - U.S stocks were little changed
at the open Thursday as the market digested mixed data that
showed a rise in weekly jobless claims and
stronger-than-expected retail sales for November. 
The Dow Jones industrial average fell 20.56 points or
0.13 percent, to 15,822.97, the S  500 lost 1.99 points
or 0.11 percent, to 1,780.23 and the Nasdaq Composite 
added 3.471 points or 0.09 percent, to 4,007.284.
