

Dec 12 (Reuters) - Shares of Hilton Worldwide Holdings Inc
, the world's largest hotel operator, rose 7 percent in
their New York Stock Exchange debut on  Thursday. 
Shares opened at $21.30 after Blackstone Group LP 
company raised over $2.3 billion in the year's second-biggest
IPO. Hilton shares priced at $20 on Wednesday night, within the
expected range of $18 to $21. The company priced 117.6 million
shares.
