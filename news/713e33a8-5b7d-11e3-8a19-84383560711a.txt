

LAGOS  Dec 2 (Reuters) - Nigeria's Oando  
said on Monday that it has delayed the completion of its $1.79
billion deal to buy Conocophillips' Nigerian assets by
two months to January. 
Oando has been aggressively seeking equity and bank loan
funding to secure funds for the deal's completion.
