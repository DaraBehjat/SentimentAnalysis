     "Although there are several hurdles still to climb, including a skeptical U.S. Congress, the deal has already seen an initial impact in markets," said Simon Smith, chief economist at FxPro Financial Services in London.     On Sunday, diplomats made history when Iran and six world powers reached  an agreement  over the country's nuclear program.     The deal brokered between the U.S., U.K., France, Russia, China and Germany curtails Iran's ability to develop a nuclear weapon.     Iran has been hard hit in recent years as various sanctions have hurt the oil-rich economy and led to  staggering inflation levels .
