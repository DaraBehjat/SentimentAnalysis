

NEW YORK  Dec 3 (Reuters) - U.S. stocks opened lower on
Tuesday, with the S  500 down for a third consecutive session,
on concerns the Federal Reserve may begin to reduce its stimulus
earlier than some had anticipated. 
The Dow Jones industrial average fell 25.95 points or
0.16 percent, to 15,982.82, the S  500 lost 2.96 points
or 0.16 percent, to 1,797.94 and the Nasdaq Composite 
dropped 6.074 points or 0.15 percent, to 4,039.186.
