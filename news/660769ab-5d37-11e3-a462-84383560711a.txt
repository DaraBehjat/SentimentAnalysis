

MEXICO CITY  Dec 4 (Reuters) - Mexico's biggest retailer,
Wal-Mart de Mexico said on Wednesday sales at its Mexican stores
open at least a year rose by 0.4 percent in November compared
with the same month in 2012. 
Total sales in Mexico rose by 5.3 percent, the company said.
