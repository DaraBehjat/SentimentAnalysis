

TORONTO, Dec 3 (Reuters) - Canada's main stock index opened
lower on Tuesday as shares of Bank of Montreal dropped
after the company reported quarterly results and concerns about
the U.S. Federal Reserve's bond buying program weighed on
overall sentiment.
    The Toronto Stock Exchange's S /TSX composite index
 was down 52.05 points, or 0.39 percent, at 13,367.52
shortly after the open.
