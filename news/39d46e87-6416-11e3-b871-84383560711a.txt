

NEW YORK  Dec 13 (Reuters) - Wall Street rose modestly at
the open on Friday, putting the S  500 on track to snap a
three-day decline, though market participants remained cautious
ahead of a Federal Reserve policy meeting next week. 
The Dow Jones industrial average rose 25.4 points or
0.16 percent, to 15,764.83, the S  500 gained 4.31
points or 0.24 percent, to 1,779.81 and the Nasdaq Composite
 added 17.037 points or 0.43 percent, to 4,015.439.
