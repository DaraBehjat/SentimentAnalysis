

Copper and gold miner PT Newmont Nusa Tenggara said it was ready
to process copper concentrate in Indonesia, said Chief Executive
Martiono Hadianto. 
The Indonesian unit of Newmont Mining Corp signed a
conditional sales purchase agreement with PT Indosmelt to supply
copper concentrate, starting in 2016. * Note: Reuters has not verified this story and does not
vouch for its accuracy.
