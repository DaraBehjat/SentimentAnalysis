

PT Fastfood Indonesia, the franchise holder of KFC
brand, plans to spend up to 350 billion rupiah ($29.26 million)
to open 30 new outlets in 2014 and renovate up to 60 existing
stores, said Finance Director Justinus D. Juwono. 
The company expects 2014 revenue to rise by up to 12 percent
to 4.53 trillion rupiah from this year's forecast of 4.12
trillion rupiah. * Note: Reuters has not verified this story and does not
vouch for its accuracy.
